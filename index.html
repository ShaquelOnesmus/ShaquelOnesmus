My Work

I am a highly accomplished actor who has graced both the small and big screens with my exceptional performances. My notable appearances include roles in acclaimed television series such as "Zora" on Citizen TV, "Paa Season 2," "Crime & Justice," "Single Kiasi," "White Gold," and "Kasri." In addition to my acting prowess, I am a versatile voice-over artist, lending my captivating voice to various projects. As a commercial fashion model, I have had the privilege of representing the esteemed clothing brand Deevown Collection. Moreover, my talent has been showcased in prominent advertisements for renowned brands like Kapa Oil, Shell (billboard), Jumia, and Infinix Note 10. My extensive experience and diverse range of work demonstrate my unwavering dedication to my craft and my ability to excel across different mediums.
